A Particular Set of Skills

Projects are a way I try to scaffold my skills into deliverables. More importantly, it can be downright cool, here are a few of them.

Springboard Judge

Olympic-style diving, amazing to watch -- but to practice alone? Fuhgettaboutit. What springboard judge attempts to do is to provide machine learning analysis of athlete's form/body part position. It aims to predict good dives vs bad dives in real time.

This can also be expanded to life-or-death scenarios like boxing or martial arts where anomalies in these patterns could be a sign of neurological failure and stop a match based on reliable evidence.

Stable Eyes

Have you heard of liftware? Well imagine the same thing but for a phone view. Stable eyes is a view stabilization correction for android hoping to combat chronic diseases like parkinsons when doing everyday activities.

Activity Recognizer

How does my Pebble/Fitbit watch know that I'm running? Well through a bit of accelerometer pattern detection machine learning one can pretty accurately predict what you are up to with a little bit of x, y, and z data.

Super Debugger

Like debuggers? Don't like GDB's Complexity? Super Debugger is an optimized debugger supporting basic watchpoints, breakpoints, and a readability layer for ease of use.
